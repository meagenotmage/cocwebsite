Student Organizations

BROADCAST GUILD

An organization in the College of Communication for Broadcasting Students. Organized to help and educate future media practitioners to be eloquent, effective, and critical communicators.

Learn More
THE MEDIATOR

The Mediator is the official student publication of the College of Communication - West Visayas State University Main Campus. We do hereby abide the Republic Act No. 7079, or the "Campus Journalism Act of 1991". We live with the truth and justice, bearing the core of HOPS: Humility, Objectivity, Passion, and Service.

Learn More
YOUNG JOURNALIST'S SOCIETY

The Young Journalists’ Society (YJS) is the flagship student organization from the Journalism Division of the College of Communication that is committed in providing opportunities for communication students to engage, learn, and lead.

Learn More
STUDIOSITAS

Studiositas Honor Society is an organization for the Academic Achievers of West Visayas State University College of Communication. Our vision is to continually inspire students to excel, empowering them to become effective instruments for societal advancement in the future. We are committed to instilling a strong sense of academic distinction and ethical standards among communication students, preparing them to be leaders who uphold the highest ideals of integrity and excellence.

Learn More
DEVELOPMENT COMMUNICATOR'S SOCIETY

WVSU Development Communicators’ Society is an independent student-led organization composed of DevCom students at West Visayas State University. The organization aims to mirror out the values of Development Communication of being PRAGMATIC, PURPOSIVE, and VALUE-LADEN, and equip its members with essential, technical, and necessary skills and experiences to bring the margin to the center.

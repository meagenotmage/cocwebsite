THE COLLEGE OF COMMUNICATIONS STUDENT COUNCIL

Vision

Membentuk pembelajar yang akidatul karimah, berilmu, beretika, berwawasan lingkungan untuk menuju pentas dunia.

Mission

  • Mewujudkan pendidikan dengan keteladanan
  • Mengembangkan budaya belajar dengan didasari pada kecintaan terhadap ilmu pengetahuan
  • Meningkatkan fasilitas sekolah menuju sekolah bersih, sehat dan berwawasan lingkungan

College of Communication Student Council

  1. The College of Communication Student Council (COC SC) represents the College of - Communication students, advocating for their needs and rights. It fosters responsible - student engagement, provides a platform for student voices, champions academic freedom, strives for educational reform, promotes unity, and cultivates social awareness and national consciousness.
